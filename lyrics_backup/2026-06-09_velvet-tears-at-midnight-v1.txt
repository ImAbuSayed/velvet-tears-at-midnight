[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Midnight falls and we’re the only ones awake
I feel the shiver in the promises we make
Your heavy breathing pulls me deeper in the spark
I want to love you right here in the dark

[Verse 1]
We left the headlights shining on the empty street
To find a quiet place where both our shadows meet
You’re sliding closer and my hands begin to trace
The gentle curves and the beauty of your face

[Pre-Chorus]
The coffee’s cold but the fire is burning bright
I see the craving in your eyes tonight
Don’t say a word, just let my fingers speak
This kind of magic makes me wonderfully weak

[Chorus]
Oh, let these velvet tears at midnight fall like rain
A slow-burn passion washing over every vein
I want to memorize the way you call my name
We're softly burning in a beautiful and wild flame

[Post-Chorus]
[Whispered] (Hold me closer, don’t you let me go)
[Whispered] (We’ll take it nice and slow)

[Verse 2]
I hear the wooden floorboards creaking as you sway
You wear my flannel shirt to chase the chill away
The window’s open and the country breeze is sweet
But nothing's sweeter than the places where we meet

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The shadows dance along the edges of the bed
I want to kiss away the worries in your head
Don’t say a word, just let the feeling rise
I see the universe reflected in your eyes

[Chorus]
Oh, let these velvet tears at midnight fall like rain
A slow-burn passion washing over every vein
I want to memorize the way you call my name
We're softly burning in a beautiful and wild flame

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Whispered] (Hold me closer, don’t you let me go)
[Whispered] (We’ll take it nice and slow)

[Bridge]
A tender confession while the world is fast asleep
I'll give you secrets that are only mine to keep
I never knew a love so wonderfully sweet
But you're the melody that makes Saeed complete

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (Come a little closer to me now)
[Spoken] (Let me show you what my heart is beating for)

[Final Chorus]
Oh, let these velvet tears at midnight fall like rain
A slow-burn passion washing over every vein
I want to memorize the way you call my name
We're softly burning in a beautiful and wild flame
